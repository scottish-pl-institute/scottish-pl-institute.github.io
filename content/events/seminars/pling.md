PLInG is a programming languages seminar organised by the University of Edinburgh. PLInG usually takes place on Wednesdays at 11am, although times vary to suit speakers and room availability.  To subscribe from outside the university, please see [the instructions for subscribing by email](https://information-services.ed.ac.uk/computing/comms-and-collab/email/lists/sympa/subscribe), using the list name `inf-pl-interest`.  Talk announcements are also sent to the seminars channel on Zulip.
